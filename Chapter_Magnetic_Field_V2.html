Magnetic Field

Quick idea: Magnetic fields act on moving charges. The force direction is set by a cross product.

Lorentz force (magnetic part)

The magnetic force on a charge \(q\) moving with velocity \(\vec{v}\) in a magnetic field \(\vec{B}\) is:

\(\vec{F} = q\,\vec{v}\times\vec{B}\)
